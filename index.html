Convocation Office

The Convocation is a statutory body and represents the largest constituency of the Eastern Statistical Training Center (EASTC) â€“ its alumni. Established in 2018, the Convocation provides EASTC graduates with representation in the affairs of the institution. From its inception, it was recognized that graduates form a crucial component of the ESTC community. The membership of the Convocation is inclusive of all Departments and Faculties within ESTC. The role of the Convocation is to discuss and express its opinions on any matters concerning the institution. This platform enables alumni to have a significant voice in ESTC's operations. In fulfilling this mandate, Convocation members participate in various institutional committees, ensuring the alumni perspective is considered in the decision-making process. Furthermore, the Convocation fosters a lifelong connection between its members and EASTC, benefitting both the institution and its current and future members.

Convocation Executive Committee

This is an executive organ of the Convocation chaired by the President. It is a decision making body and is responsible for carrying out the main functions of the Convocation. The composition of the Convocation Executive Committee (ECC) consist of The President, The Vice President, The Vice Chancellor, DVC - ARC, DVC â€“ PFA (secretary to the Convocation), and other members elected during Annual General Meeting

Convocation Activities Committee

This committee deliberate on and determine three year activity action plan of the Convocation [of routine and/or innovative nature] and, with approval of the ECC, may seek to review or follow-up on annual basis in the interest of the entire alumni and the University, including maintaining and updating of the Convocation, as well as report on such activities at the annual general meetings (AGM) of the Convocation members; Maintains calendar of meetings in time for a reporting to the ECC as may be scheduled.

About us

Established in 1965 in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, EASTC trains statisticians for East African nations. It expanded into a regional institution serving 19 African countries and now offers degree programs in Official Statistics.
